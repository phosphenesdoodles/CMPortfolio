Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Sijia She, a student at Georgia Tech with a passion for animation and game design. My artistic journey began with cartoons from my childhood, like Steven Universe, Adventure Time, and Winx, which sparked my love for vibrant colors and creative storytelling. I'm proficient in a variety of art mediums, including oil paint, watercolor, and digital art. I'm particularly drawn to bright colors and maximalist designs, which often inspire my work. I've included my favorite examples of art I've made in the following sections. My goal is to bring my love for colorful, dynamic design into the world of animation or game development.

I've included two of my favorite animations projects. The first was drawn in Procreate and rigged in Live2DCubism. The second was contructed fully in Blender. I made both projects from scratch, starting from character design to the final rigged models.

Here is a collection of my favorite traditional pieces. The gallery showcases a variety of mediums: oil paint, acrylic paint, watercolors, ceramic, and a couple mixed media pieces.

This is a collection of my favorite digital pieces, all done in Procreate. In this collection, I have a mixture of commissioned art, art I've drawn for friends, and personal projects.
